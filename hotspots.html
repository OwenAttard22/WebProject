Tourist Hotspots

Explore some of Lyon's most popular tourist attractions below.

HOTSPOTS

Popular Attractions

Vieux Lyon

Historic Walking Areas

Lyon's historic section is full of old buildings and a traditional French atmosphere.

Basilique Notre Dame de Fourviere

Architectural Buildings

This Basilica, constructed in the late 19th century, has an ornate interior to contrast its grave exterior.

Colline de Fourviere

Historic Walking Area

This hill was the location of Lyon's original Roman settlement and still is home to ancient ruins from that era.

Pearse Lyon Distillery

Distillery

The Pearse Lyons Distillery produces some of Ireland’s finest small batch Irish whiskies and celebrates the Irish tradition of storytelling on each guided tour.

Parc de la Tête d'Or

Park

Come enjoy a moment of relaxation in a wooded and natural setting. A double course of 18 tracks awaits you.

Traboules du Vieux Lyon

Landmark

Traboules, these shortcuts in the form of internal passage, allow communication from one street to another by crossing buildings.

La Place Bellecour

Landmark

Place Bellecour is a large square in the centre of Lyon, France, to the north of the Ainay district.

The Presqu'île

Neighbourhood

The Presqu'île is the central part of the City of Lyon, France.
